You Have 60 Seconds, to stop the Loop, and save time!

Paradox is a time-travelling 3d puzzle platformer in which you are your own worst enemy.

Play Video

In the Game "Paradox" you play as a being known only as Onyx, a time traveller who mysteriously got stuck in an endless loop between time and space. The only way for her to escape the infinate cycle is to fix time itself, in order to complete her mission she must collect all the fragmented “Hours” throughout the various loop cycles and ultimatly leave the empty worlds behind.

However, she has already done this before and it is up to the player to guide her through the infinate possibilities of past-selves and minefields of paradox's to stop the endless cycle and save time.

Features

Restore Time by

Collecting Hourglasses

The "Hours" are fragmented, they must be collected in order for the loop cycle to end. Collecting all 12 grants you passage out of the current loop and into another, however there is a catch. Each "Hour" collected you are forcefully rewound to the beginning!!

Watch your step!

Avoid your Past-Selves

Each step you take, each action you do, counts! each time an "Hour" has been saved an image of yourself appears, which projects all the actions you did to get to the previously collected hourglass. Touching your past-self will cause a catastrophic dimensional paradox!

Help yourself

You're on your own

Quite literally your on your own, but being alone can help you. Utilize different loop cycles to reach un-reachable places, move obstacles out of the way, or even reveal new areas! Nobody is here to help, just you!

Time matters

You Have till midnight and its only midday!

12 hours seems like a long time, but not when your in a dimensional time loop! As you move, time still goes on, but at an accelerating rate. Once the clock hits midnight all your progress is wasted and you are rewound back to the beginning of your current cycle.

Fate has been determined

Avoid the Paradox!

Onyx's fate has been sealed and progress awaits, she cannot fail by any means, any inclination to do so, causes a paradox. So watch your step, avoid yourself and make sure you get there in time!
