About

My name is Cameron. I am an innovative media designer that combines a multitude of techniques and software to create content that is both entertaining and appealing to a wide range of audiences. The box will never be my home, and the creations of my dreams will be a reality. I live to entertain, to tell a story, to show the future.

My work could be a whole range of things, websites to Games, Animations to Sound, documenteries to random snippits of code that only do one thing. It doesnt matter what it is, all that matters, is my one golden rule, to tell us a message, no matter how simple or complicated it is.
